All I hope  
For life to be  
A symphony  
In harmony  
Perfect pitch  
A lovely little masterpiece

A song born soft like lullabies  
Woodwinds and brass, strings intertwined  
Flutes sound as bright as golden shine  
Bells that ring, a chord of chimes  
Choirs sing in tones divine  
Cellos deep, like strongest spine  
Snares that keep the pace in line  
Crescendos rise at perfect time  
Bursting out, each trumpet whines  
A boundless bass that splits the sky  
Behind conductor’s stick, a storyline

Life, hear my song  
Pick your place and play along  
Weave in notes that shape for me  
A lovely little masterpiece

--Connor
