Ghosts don’t like to come out on nice days like this  
With sunny rays like this  
A burning blaze like this  
They’d rather find a corner in a shed out back  
Where it’s dead out back  
So much dread out back  
Then they wait for quiet just to follow you  
Try to swallow you  
Make a hollow you

You’re good at hiding all the pain you have  
Endless rain you have  
Relentless shame you have  
Don’t you want to see your friends again  
Not pretend again  
Make amends again  
It’s killed the things that mean the most to you  
When it’s close to you  
You’re a ghost of you

You should know we all have things to hide  
Many masks to hide  
A troubled past to hide  
You should know your emotions aren’t all you are  
In the dark you are  
But still a spark you are  
You should know this before you fade away  
Fight the shade away  
Don’t push aid away

Leave the shed door open wide  
Let the sun inside  
Watch your shadows hide  
Then you'll find yourself in a better place  
Working every day  
To chase the ghosts away

--Connor
