A rope is pulled taut to remove all the slack  
When two people are fighting  
There’s no length to give back  
This tug of war is a game  
That’s not between friends  
You’ll find love works the best  
When you both pull from one end

So stop weaving your rope  
With the whole thing in mind  
Your futures are built  
One tangled thread at a time  
Frayed spools of loose twine  
Won’t make a good rope  
When you pull from both ends  
It’ll still snap despite hope

--Connor
