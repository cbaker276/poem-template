Shine bright silver moon  
I’m barely hanging in  
I’ve waited since the break of dawn  
To rest with you again

Sit still sleepy traveler  
Lay down your weary head  
For I’m so full of light  
And you’re so full of dread

Shine bright silver moon  
I dread that I must sleep  
The day has stolen all I have  
And left me worn and weak

Sit still sleepy traveler  
You must learn to love the sun  
It gives you light to live your life  
For the things that must be done

Shine bright silver moon  
But it takes too much from me  
It burns away the time we have  
And leaves you hard to see

Sit still sleepy traveler  
If I were here all day  
You’d miss the golden morning sun  
And wish my night away

Goodnight silver moon  
I know you speak the truth  
The sun will always have its place  
But I still wait for you

Sleep well, weary traveler  
The sun will be back soon  
For those who bear the weight of day  
Are held up by the moon

--Connor
