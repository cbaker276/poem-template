Shine bright silver light  
I’m barely holding on  
I’ve waited since the break of dawn  
To rest with you tonight

Sit still sleepy traveler  
Lay down your weary head  
Hear crickets sing and train horns ring  
Escape from all your dread

Shine bright silver light  
I fear only the sun  
When it’s high, up in the sky  
There are things that must be done

Sit still sleepy traveler  
Don’t rush your life away  
The slower that you take it  
The longer it will stay

Shine bright silver light  
I hear the words you speak  
But you cannot relate to me  
There are promises I keep

Sit still sleepy traveler  
I know you think you’re right  
But even I must keep my word  
And make it through the night

Goodnight silver light  
Your patience guides my way  
You rise and fall without a rush  
And help me through the day

Sleep well, weary traveler  
The sun will come back soon  
For those who bear the weight of day  
Are held up by the moon

--Connor
