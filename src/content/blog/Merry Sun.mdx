Meet me at the creek  
Or in the meadow  
Under hill and over hill  
Or resting near in shadows

Meet me at the window  
In a little coffee shop  
Or meet me through the trees  
At a hidden hiking spot

Meet me as a flower  
That’s reaching for the sky  
Or meet me as a toad  
On a lily floating by

Meet me when it’s late  
As you’re barely holding on  
I’ll ask the moon to meet with me  
Until you rise at dawn

Meet me merry sun  
Make me happy in your sight  
Find me in your travels  
Let me hold on to your light

For all the times you meet with me  
I hope you meet things too  
The hills and sills in little shops  
And meadows you’ve helped bloom

I hope you meet the tallest mountains  
As the day comes falling down  
Linger on the skyline  
As you set behind the clouds

Make friends with all the fish  
As you’re swallowed by the sea  
And rise once more tomorrow  
To meet anywhere with me

--Connor
